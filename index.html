The Time Has Come!

Rejoice all people from far and wide for the new king of felines has heralded his appearance on this web of interconnection. Verily, I say unto thee, King Alphinaud Francois has a realm of his own in which you may gaze upon his majestic countenance, marvel at his uncanny feats, and reflect upon his mercy and charity. Truly these are jubilant times!
